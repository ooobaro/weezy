Asaba Urban Drainage Design and Construction

Aims and objectives of the project

To develop the most optimal, efficient and effective floodwater control structures that would safely convey the rainfall precipitation that generates runoff from the catchment basins within the State capital, Asaba. To provide answer to non-functionality of existing drainage facilities To design the most economical, efficient and effective floodwater control structures that will safely evacuate the precipitated rainfall runoff from the environment to the natural discharge points, with the least impact on the citizens and the environment in general.
