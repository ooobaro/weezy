7 THINGS YOU NEED TO KNOW BEFORE HIRING A STORMWATER CONSTRUCTION COMPANY

29 SEPTEMBER 2022

DEPROF

By Engr. John Cee Onwualu(FNSE)

When it comes to stormwater drainage construction, it is important to hire a reputable and experienced company. The process is complex and requires a number of steps to ensure the job is done correctly and efficiently.

If you are considering hiring a stormwater drainage construction company, there are a few things you need to know before making a decision.

Knowing the details of the project, understanding the regulations and permit requirements, and assessing the credentials of the company you are hiring are just a few of the important factors to consider.

In this article, we take a look at 7 key things you should know before hiring a stormwater drainage construction company.

From understanding the scope of the project to making sure you’re getting the best value, this article provides you with the information you need to make an informed decision and ensure you hire the best company for the job.

1. Ensure they are fully licensed and insured

When considering engaging a storm water drainage construction company, it is essential to check that the company is fully licensed and insured.

Ensure that the company’s license is up-to-date and that it carries the necessary insurance. Doing so will provide assurance that the company is reputable and that any liability issues are covered.

Furthermore, verify that the company’s employees are qualified and have all the required certifications to work in the storm water drainage construction industry.

2. Verify they have a good reputation

Before hiring a stormwater drainage construction company, make sure you check their reputation by researching reviews and client testimonials.

See how long they have been in business and take into consideration any certifications or qualifications that could ensure they are experienced in the field.

You should also make sure the company is licensed and insured in your state. Doing your due diligence can save you a lot of hassle in the future.


3. Confirm They Have Experience with Similar Projects

Before committing to a stormwater drainage construction company, it is important to make sure they have experience with similar projects. Ask to see a portfolio of their previous works and inquire about their experience with drainage systems in your area.

It is also a good idea to ask for references from other customers, so you can get a clear sense of their professionalism and quality of work. Doing your due diligence upfront is the best way to ensure you are working with a reputable drainage construction company that is capable of providing the results you are looking for.

For more clarification, request a detailed list of their services. Ask the company to provide you with a comprehensive list of services, personnel, including the types of projects they specialize in and have handled. This information can help you make sure the company you choose is right for your needs and that they have the skills and experience to complete your project.

4. Check That They Provide a Detailed Proposal

Before hiring a storm water drainage construction company, make sure they provide a detailed proposal that outlines the project management in full.

This should include an overview of the proposed construction, the scope definition and work breakdown, the plan schedule for completion, estimated costs, quality assurance and quality control plans, list of personnel to be involved, risk management plan, and procurement processes in the project. You will also identify the stakeholders and their involvement.

A comprehensive proposal will help you understand the scope of the project and ensure that the company is qualified to complete the work. Additionally, it should provide you with a clear idea of the timeline and budget, allowing you to make an informed decision.

Getting clarification on the estimated costs is important. It is important you understand all the costs associated with the project construction and that what has been estimated is reasonable for the scope of the job.

Ask for a full breakdown of the costs and any other costs you might have to pay. It’s also important to get clarity on who is responsible for the payment of any subcontractors or suppliers. Make sure to factor in any additional fees or taxes that may be applicable.


5. Consider Their Approach to Storm Water Drainage

When looking at companies that build stormwater drainage systems, it’s important to think about how they design and build drainage systems.

The right design, equipment application and materials can make a big difference in the long-term efficiency and cost of a drainage system.

Also, the company should know about any local government rules about storm water drainage in order to make sure they are followed.

Ask potential companies about their experience and ask for examples of drainage systems they have constructed in the past. This will help you make an informed decision.


6. Make sure they can accommodate your timeline

It’s important to select a stormwater drainage construction company that can meet your timeline. Find out if they have a timeline that is realistic and achievable.

Also, ensure that they have the personnel and resources to complete the project within the timeframe you have set. Equally, be certain that they have a plan for dealing with any unexpected delays or issues that may arise.


7. Ensure they use the right tools and materials

The seventh point to consider when hiring a stormwater drainage construction company is that they should use the right equipment, experienced personnel, and quality materials for the job.

This means making sure that the company is using a healthy equipment, experienced personnel, use of quality materials and applying good techniques that meet the quality standard requirement, as provided in the codes of engineering practice.

Working with a company that is not using the right tools and materials can result in costly delays and possible safety risks. When considering hiring a stormwater drainage construction company, be sure to ask about the materials and tools they use and if they are up to date and compliant with current regulations.

Final Thoughts

In conclusion, having a reliable stormwater drainage construction company is essential to your project’s success. Finding the right one requires research, planning, and due diligence.

With these 7 considerations in mind, you can feel confident in your ability to choose the right stormwater drainage construction company for your needs.
